About These Symposiums

The Annual PDF-RS Symposium is a prestigious academic event organized by the Research Scholars at the Stat-Math Unit of the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore. It provides a vibrant platform for Postdoctoral Fellows and Research Scholars to present their ongoing research and foster academic collaboration.
