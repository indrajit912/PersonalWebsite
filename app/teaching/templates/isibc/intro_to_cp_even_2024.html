Workshop on Completely Positive Maps

Term
Even Semester 2024
Duration
Jan 15 - Jun 04, 2024
Lecturers
Indrajit Ghosh, Gahin Maiti, S. Nayak

Overview:

This workshop centers around the theoretical exploration of completely positive maps and its associated domain. In the initial sessions, we will delve into the fundamentals of quantum theory, encompassing both mathematical principles and introductory physical aspects. Subsequently, we will pivot to the foundational concepts of quantum information, covering topics such as registers/states, quantum channels, and measurements. Following this groundwork, our focus will shift towards a comprehensive understanding of Completely Positive Maps, encompassing discussions on Dilation Theorems, Arveson's Extension Theorem, and the \(C^*\)-tensor product. The latter part of the workshop will introduce participants to Completely Bounded Maps and their interconnected areas.

Lecture Timings: 10:00 AM - 04:15 PM with breaks (11:00-11:15 AM coffee, 01:00-02:00 PM lunch and 03:00-03:15 PM tea)
